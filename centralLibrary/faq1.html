FAQ

The library is typically open from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Please check for holiday closures or special timings.

You can register for membership by filling out an application form that is available on the webpage of Central Library under the Downloads section. One passport size photo is also required. Students may need to show their college ID.

The library offers a wide collection including books, journals, newspapers, research papers across various disciplines.

Yes, members can borrow books. The standard loan period is a minimum of 20 days, with the possibility of renewal unless the item is reserved by another user.

Yes, overdue items may incur a fine per day. Please refer to the library's borrowing policy for current rates.

Yes, free internet and Wi-Fi access are available for registered users within the library premises.

Yes, the library offers research journals, reference services, access to online databases, and reading space specifically for students and researchers.

The library welcomes donations. Please contact the assistant librarian before donating to ensure the materials meet current collection needs.

You can reach the library help desk in person or contact via email at centrallibrary@nitmanipur.ac.in . Staff members are available to assist with your queries.
