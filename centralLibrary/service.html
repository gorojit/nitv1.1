Welcome to Department of Library

Services

Our library is committed to supporting academic excellence, research advancement, and a culture of reading and inquiry. We offer a wide range of services and resources designed to meet the diverse needs of students, researchers, and faculty members.

1. Circulation Services - The library provides efficient circulation services, including borrowing and returning of books, renewals, and reservations. Registered users can borrow materials as per the lending policy, ensuring easy access to essential academic resources.
2. Reading Area - To foster a quiet and comfortable study environment, the library offers a dedicated reading area with 34 well-equipped seats. This space supports both individual study and focused academic engagement.
3. Plagiarism Check Using Turnitin and Drillbit - Ensuring academic integrity is a core priority. The library offers plagiarism checking services through Turnitin, a leading originality checking software and Drillbit. Faculty and research scholars can avail of this service to maintain high ethical standards in their academic work.
4. PhD Theses Submission: Shodhganga - PhD scholars are required to submit their approved theses to Shodhganga, a digital repository maintained by INFLIBNET. This platform allows scholars to make their work accessible to the wider academic community while complying with UGC guidelines.
5. Research Synopsis Submission: Shodhgangotri - In alignment with research transparency and progress tracking, scholars can also submit their research synopses through Shodhgangotri. This repository showcases proposed research projects and contributes to nationwide research visibility.
6. E-Resources - The library provides access to an extensive collection of electronic resources to support teaching, learning, and research:

  • One Nation, One Subscription (ONOS)
  • E-books and Journals from reputed academic publishers including: Springer Nature, Taylor and Francis, Elsevier and Pearson.
