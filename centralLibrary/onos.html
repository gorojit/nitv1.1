One Nation One Subscription (ONOS) Initiative at NIT Manipur Central Library

We are excited to announce that the Central Library of NIT Manipur is now part of the One Nation One Subscription (ONOS) initiative. This national initiative aims to simplify access to digital services across the country by providing citizens with a single subscription to connect to multiple platforms.

With the integration of ONOS, students, faculty, and staff of NIT Manipur can now enjoy seamless access to all digital library resources, including e-books, journals, and databases, through IP-based access. This initiative reduces the need for multiple accounts and streamlines the process of accessing educational materials, making research and learning more efficient and convenient.

We encourage all users to take full advantage of this new, simplified access to enhance their academic and research experience.
