Vision

Towards a knowledge hub of excellence in education, research, innovation, entrepreneurship and nurturing skilled professionals committed to sustainable ecosystem.

Mission

  • To impart quality education in engineering technology and allied fields to produce competent and ethical professionals.
  • To foster a culture of research and innovation that addresses emerging challenges and contributes to technological growth.
  • To promote entrepreneurship and leadership skills for developing sustainable solutions, socio-economic growth and global well being.

About NIT Manipur

NIT Manipur began its first session on 2nd August 2010 with three engineering branches: Electrical, Electronics and Communication, and Computer Science at Government Polytechnic, Takyelpat. It now operates from its permanent campus at Langol, Imphal, spread over 341.5 acres, with two additional branches, Mechanical and Civil Engineering.

Director's Message

Prof. D V L N Somayajulu Director, NIT Manipur

Prof. Somayajulu has 35 years of experience in teaching, research, consultancy, and outreach works in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. He joined as Director of National Institute of Technology Manipur on June 1st, 2024. His academic career started in NIT(REC) Warangal in the month of September 1988 and promoted as Professor of Computer Science & Engineering in 2006.
